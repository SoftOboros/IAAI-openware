# Analog Working Group

This working group focuses on analog AI hardware technologies, including memory-based computing architectures, weight encoding methods, and emerging analog AI accelerator standards.

## Table of Contents

### **Overview & Foundational Documents**
- [A Student's Guide to the Three Flavors of Analog AI Memory](./Transactions/A%20Student's%20Guide%20to%20the%20Three%20Flavors%20of%20Analog%20AI%20Memory.md) - An accessible introduction explaining how PCM, Flash, and FeFET memory technologies work for analog AI, using analogies to illustrate each technology's key advantages and challenges.
- [Forging the Future of AI: A Strategic Analysis of FeFET, Flash and PCM for Analog In-Memory Computing](./Transactions/Forging%20the%20Future%20of%20AI_%20A%20Strategic%20Analysis%20of%20FeFET,%20Flash%20and%20PCM%20for%20Analog%20In-Memory%20Computing.md) - A comprehensive technical analysis of the three leading non-volatile memory candidates for analog AI, dissecting fundamental physics, reliability challenges, and scaling pathways to guide strategic technology investment decisions.
- [Proposal for Unified Signal and Metrology Standards for Analog AI Hardware](./Transactions/Proposal%20for%20Unified%20Signal%20and%20Metrology%20Standards%20for%20Analog%20AI%20Hardware.md) - Outlines a comprehensive cross-technology standardization framework for JEDEC and IEEE addressing signal specifications, metrology standards, and interface protocols to ensure reliable, scalable deployment of analog AI hardware.

### Transactions (Discussion Documents)
- [Analog AI Neurotrophic Structure Research](./Transactions/Analog%20AI%20Neurotrophic%20Structure%20Research.md) - Explores biological structural plasticity principles and their translation into analog AI hardware through dynamic network topology algorithms, positioning topology as the primary carrier of information in low-precision analog systems.
- [Analog AI Standards and Regulation Research](./Transactions/Analog%20AI%20Standards%20and%20Regulation%20Research.md) - Addresses the metrology, standardization, and regulatory landscape for analog AI weight encoding, covering NIST initiatives, JEDEC standards, ISO/IEC frameworks, and EU AI Act implications for high-risk analog systems.
- [Analog AI Weight Encoding Report](./Transactions/Analog%20AI%20Weight%20Encoding%20Report.md) - Comprehensive analysis of advanced weight encoding methodologies including differential pair encoding, bit-slicing architectures, and techniques like VECOM and SWIPE for improving precision and reliability in analog computing.
- [Analog AI Weight Encoding Report-2](./Transactions/Analog%20AI%20Weight%20Encoding%20Report-2.md) - Synthesizes state-of-the-art weight encoding physics and programming algorithms including Gradient Descent-Based Programming and Tiki-Taka, with detailed technology roadmap projections through 2025.
- [Analog AI Weight Encoding Research](./Transactions/Analog%20AI%20Weight%20Encoding%20Research.md) - Investigates the "Analog Manifold" continuous physical state space for encoding neural weights across diverse technologies including memristors, ferroelectric effects, magnetic spintronics, and photonic systems.
- [Analog AI Weight Encoding Research Analysis](./Transactions/Analog%20AI%20Weight%20Encoding%20Research%20Analysis.md) - Detailed comparative analysis of input encoding schemes (PAM vs. PWM vs. bit-serial) and their trade-offs, evaluating the ADC/readout bottleneck and mitigation strategies.
- [Analog Memory Technologies Report Deep Dive](./Transactions/Analog%20Memory%20Technologies%20Report%20Deep%20Dive.md) - Examines ferroelectric (FeFET) and Flash/floating-gate technologies, detailing polarization dynamics, nucleation-limited switching, charge-trapping mechanisms, and 3D scaling challenges.
- [Analog ReRAM Research Deep Dive](./Transactions/Analog%20ReRAM%20Research%20Deep%20Dive.md) - Comprehensive coverage of resistive RAM physics including VCM, ECM, and filament conductivity mechanisms, examining how stochasticity and drift challenge precision analog encoding.
- [PCM Analog AI Accelerator Deep Dive](./Transactions/PCM%20Analog%20AI%20Accelerator%20Deep%20Dive.md) - Exhaustive technical analysis of Phase-Change Memory for analog weight storage, covering nucleation dynamics, drift physics, superlattice innovations, and benchmark performance on Transformer models.
- [Phase-Change Memory Analog AI Research](./Transactions/Phase-Change%20Memory%20Analog%20AI%20Research.md) - Synthesizes thermodynamics of amorphous/crystalline transitions in PCM, detailing crystallization kinetics and comprehensive mitigation strategies for conductance drift to achieve reliable long-term analog inference.
